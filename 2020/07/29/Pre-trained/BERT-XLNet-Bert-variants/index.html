Posted on 2020-07-29 | In Deep Learning , Pre-trained model

RoBERTa

  1. In BERT, a 15% random mask is applied to sentences, which is a static mask. RoBERTa uses dynamic mask which samples the training corpus 10 times, and then uses 10 different masks for each sample.
  2. The experiment found that removing the NSP pre-trained task has a slight improvement in downstream tasks, so the NSP task is removed in RoBERTa.
  3. More training corpus and larger training batch.

ALBERT

The bert model is too large, and it will bring great challenges to online tasks with high speed and memory requirements. Albert uses some tricks to compress the parameters of BERT, which makes the model smaller.

  • Factorized Embedding Parameterization
    We know that a large number of parameters of the model are due to token embeddings. If the vocab is large, the model parameters will increase a lot. Assuming that V represents the total number of tokens and H represents the embeddings dimension, the entire token embeddings parameters that need to be learned are , and this matrix can be decomposed by two small matrices. , then the parameters from to , because , entire parameter is reduced a lot.

  • Cross-layer parameter sharing
    We know that bert is composed of 12 layers of transformers. Each layer of transformer has its own parameters. The entire parameter amount is relatively large. Albert shares all the layers of transformers with the first layer of transformer parameters, so the whole transformer parameters of ALBERT reduced by 12 times than BERT, and there is only one layer of transformer parameters.

  • Change NSP to SOP
    Change NSP task to sentence-order prediction (SOP) task, which means that the two adjacent sentences in the article are positive samples, and the order of the two sentences is reversed as negative samples

DistilBERT

Knowledge distillation is to use small BERT model structure (student) to learn predictive ability of some big BERT model (teacher). And using some learning strategies to obtain a smaller, faster, but have equivanlent predictive effect to a large model.

Change point:

  1. The DistilBert model and the Bert model have the same structure, but the number of layers is reduced by half, with only 6 layers of transformer structure

  2. When training, let the distilBert model try to learn the effect of the original bert model. And the design of Loss consists of three parts, two of which are related to the output of the bert model.

    • First: Use KL divergence to measure the probability distribution of the final output layer of bert and distilBert (to ensure that the probability distribution of each label predicted by the two models is as consistent as possible).

    • Second: The cos loss of the final output vector of bert and distilBert (to ensure that the output vector representation from the model is as similar as possible), represented by Lcos. XLNet

Auto Regressive model. The left-to-right language model task can only predict the next word based on the Context-before, or conversely Context-after.

  • Change point:
    Permutation Language Modeling instead of MLM in BERT.

  • Reason:

    • When predicting words, masked words are independent of each other and can not take relationship of Masked words into consideration when doing prediction.
    • BERT pre-trained and fine-tune are not consistent for Masked words. (No mask fine-tune)
